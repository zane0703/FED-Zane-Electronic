The following is a list of commonly asked questions regarding computer issues and basic troubleshooting steps to address these issues. For further help please consult our helpline

  • My PC did not turn on after I press the on button
  • My audio is not functioning
  • My monitor can't display anything

My PC did not turn on after I press the on button

Check that your power source is on and plugged in

My audio is not functioning

1) Right-click on the audio icon as shown above

2) Select "Sounds" on the the menu

3) Select "Playback" tap

4) Check if your choice of audio driver is set to default
    • Select speaker if you are using headphone jack or PC speaker
    • Select digital display audio for HDML

5) Click on "Set Default" then click "OK"

My monitor can't display anything

1) Check if your monitor are plugged in to the power source

2) Check if the display port is plugged in properly

3) If you are using dedicated gpu (e.g nvidia), check if your display port is plugged into

  the gpu instead of the motherboard
